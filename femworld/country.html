Background information:

The visualization investigates the potential correlation between GDP and femicide in these 5 countries that showed the highest number of cases within the data's study timeframe, specifically the period between 2016 and 2022. The Pearson coefficient is used to establish this correlation, and a radial axis is used graphically to represent it. Similarly, femicide statistics per country are visualized within this same timeframe and with the available data.
